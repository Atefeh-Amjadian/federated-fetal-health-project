Scenario 2 — GAN-based Augmentation (CTGAN)
==========================================

Dataset:
Fetal Health Classification (Kaggle)
https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/andrewmvd/fetal-health-classification

Protocol:
- Split into train/test with stratification
- Train CTGAN only on TRAIN
- Generate synthetic samples to balance minority classes
- Keep TEST untouched
- Train same models as Scenario 1 for fair comparison

CTGAN settings:
- epochs: 100
- batch_size: 500

Results:
1) Multinomial Logistic Regression + GAN
   Accuracy: 0.8380
   F1 macro: 0.6592

2) LightGBM + GAN
   Accuracy: 0.9389
   F1 macro: 0.8853

Notes:
- Only the training distribution was changed (augmentation).
- Models remained unchanged to isolate augmentation effect.
